About


         

Hello I’m Ryan! I recently finished grad school in Computer Science and I’m documenting my journey in machine learning. I'm also a Cofounder of InvictusBCI, a startup developing control systems for robotic prosthetics using a brain-computer-interface. My interests include computational physics, robotics, cyber-security, health-tech, brain-computer-interfacing, reinforcement learning, and entrepreneurship.

When I’m not coding or reading ML papers, I love exploring the outdoors, cooking, and photography. One of my favorite outdoor memories is commercial salmon fishing in the Gulf of Alaska. I always love meeting new people and learning new things. Please feel free to visit my github to see some of my latest coding projects. Let’s chat!
