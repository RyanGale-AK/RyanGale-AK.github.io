Hello I’m Ryan! I recently finished grad school in Computer Science and I’m documenting my journey in machine learning. When I’m not coding or reading tech blogs I love exploring the outdoors, martial arts, chess, and photography. One of my favorite outdoor memories is commercial fishing in the Gulf of Alaska. I always love meeting new people and learning new things. Please feel free to visit my github to see some of my latest coding projects. Let’s chat!
